Flow of my Project

Test Preparation

The high cost of test preparation courses reveals the great financial input of parents nowadays on their children’s education. It also shows the potential of the whole education market. As increasing venture capital noticed the opportunity and invested in the test preparation market, the future products and business environment will be mature and in line with international practice.

Consultancies

The market of overseas studying consultancies has been grown at a fast speed and the trend will continue as long as there are still many students dreaming to pursue study abroad. However, the business is lack of regulation and many common practices, such as reference letter drafting and retouch, are unethical.

Job Searching

The time gap of academic year, the limited information channels, the discrepancies between ideal salaries and the reality all deter those overseas returners. But those are exactly the strengthens or the value of those online job searching platforms.When the number of Chinese students get back to their motherland after graduating from overseas universities, this job searching market will expand in line with the former one’s speed.
