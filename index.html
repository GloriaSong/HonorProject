Capital Market of the Overseas Studies

Special Thanks to Mr. Donny Kowk who gave substaintial help during the whole process

Also, I would like to thank my interviewees who gave me solid information and insightful comments.

GET STARTED

One hundred years ago, thousands of Chinese students sponsored by government were sent overseas to study advanced techniques for future constructions of the unenlightened China. In the 21st Century, hundreds of thousand students went overseas with sponsorship from family to gain an emigration opportunity, to escape from the heavy-burdened education system, or to pursue a degree from a world top university.

The Chinese Ministry of Education reported that 523,700 Chinese students went abroad to study in 2015. The number reached a new record high and represented an increase of 13.9 percent over the 2014 level. Over 97 percent of those students were self-funded and thus they have left significant economic impact on those destination countries’ economies.

The most popular destinations they chose to study is the United States. Based on the data from the U.S. Department of Commerce, Chinese students in U.S. colleges and universities contributed US$11.43 billion to the United States’ economy in 2015.

Besides the direct expenditures in overseas countries, Chinese families also need to spent a lot on the preparations and application procedures. In the first and second tier cities of China, one of the most common products on advertisements in the metro station or in the Central Business District region is studying overseas related services provided by overseas studying consultancies and English training organizations.

Test Preparation

The high cost of test preparation courses reveals the great financial input of parents nowadays on their children’s education. It also shows the potential of the whole education market. As increasing venture capital noticed the opportunity and invested in the test preparation market, the future products and business environment will be mature and in line with international practice.

Consultancies

The market of overseas studying consultancies has been grown at a fast speed and the trend will continue as long as there are still many students dreaming to pursue study abroad. However, the business is lack of regulation and many common practices, such as reference letter drafting and retouch, are unethical.

Job Hunting

The time gap of academic year, the limited information channels, the discrepancies between ideal salaries and the reality all deter those overseas returners. But those are exactly the strengthens or the value of those online job searching platforms.When the number of Chinese students get back to their motherland after graduating from overseas universities, this job searching market will expand in line with the former one’s speed.

Yue Ma

Student from University of Alberta

This is the third year for Yue Ma, a Computer Engineering major student, to study in Canada. She is now working at a telecommunication company. “I love the freedom in Canada,” she said.

Before coming to Canada, she took the so-called hardest exam on the planet, Gaokao in China and went to a domestic 211 university at first. “I don’t want to waste my time there since people around me pays more attention on dating but not studying,” Yue complained.
